Monster Survivors

Survive against endless waves of monsters in this thrilling action-packed survival game!

Game Details

How to Play

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character around the map. Your character attacks automatically, so focus on positioning and avoiding enemies. Collect experience orbs to level up and choose new abilities or upgrade existing ones.

Features

  • Endless survival gameplay
  • Multiple character abilities and upgrades
  • Various enemy types with different behaviors
  • Progressive difficulty scaling
  • Unique power-ups and special items

Game Strategy

Focus on movement speed and area damage early on. As waves get harder, prioritize defensive abilities. Keep moving to avoid being surrounded, and try to funnel enemies into tight groups to maximize your damage output.

About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is inspired by popular roguelite survival games. Your goal is to survive as long as possible against increasingly difficult waves of monsters. The longer you survive, the stronger you become - but enemies will also grow in number and power. Test your skills and see how long you can last!
