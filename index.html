Game Overview

Monster Survivors is an exhilarating survival action game where you must fend off waves of increasingly difficult monsters. As you progress, you'll unlock powerful abilities, weapons, and skills to enhance your chances of survival.

With its addictive gameplay loop and strategic depth, Monster Survivors offers hours of entertainment as you try to survive as long as possible against the relentless monster hordes.

The game features procedurally generated levels, ensuring that no two playthroughs are exactly the same. How long can you survive?

How to Play

Controls

  • Use WASD or Arrow Keys to move your character
  • Press Space to use your special ability
  • Press E to interact with items
  • Press ESC to pause the game

Tips for Beginners

  • Keep moving to avoid being surrounded by monsters
  • Prioritize health upgrades early in your run
  • Focus on upgrading one or two weapons rather than spreading points
  • Learn enemy patterns to anticipate their movements

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Monster Survivors free to play?

Yes, Monster Survivors is completely free to play online. No downloads or purchases required.

Can I play on mobile devices?

Yes, Monster Survivors is optimized for both desktop and mobile play. The controls automatically adapt to your device.

Do I need to create an account?

No account is required to play. However, creating one allows you to save your progress and compete on the leaderboards.

What browsers are supported?

Monster Survivors works best on Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Make sure your browser is updated to the latest version.
